Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to how EditCraft works so you can place your first order with confidence.

help FAQ
What do I get with each order? add remove
One professionally edited video based on the plan you choose (Basic, Pro or Elite), plus the revision rounds and add-ons you select for that order.
How are editors assigned? add remove
In Phase 1, an admin manually assigns each order to a vetted editor based on content type, plan and deadline so workloads stay balanced and quality stays consistent.
Can I talk directly to my editor? add remove
No. All communication is via text on the platform. This keeps scopes clear, protects your time and creates a transparent history of every request and change.
How do refunds and revisions work? add remove
Refunds are only available before editing starts. After that, you use the built-in revision rounds from your plan or add extra revisions as paid add-ons.
